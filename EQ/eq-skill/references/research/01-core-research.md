# EQ情商训练 - 调研资料汇编

## 01 - 核心理论文献

### 创始文献
- Salovey, P. & Mayer, J.D. (1990). *Emotional Intelligence*. Imagination, Cognition and Personality, 9(3), 185-211.
- Goleman, D. (1995). *Emotional Intelligence: Why It Can Matter More Than IQ*. Bantam Books.
- Goleman, D. (1998). *Working with Emotional Intelligence*. Bantam Books.

### 理论发展
- Mayer, J.D., Salovey, P., & Caruso, D.R. (2000). *Models of emotional intelligence*. In R.J. Sternberg (Ed.), Handbook of Intelligence.
- Petrides, K.V. & Furnham, A. (2001). *Trait emotional intelligence*. European Journal of Personality.

### MSCEIT

- 技术手册与信效度报告

### EQ-i 2.0
- Bar-On, R. (1997). *The Emotional Quotient Inventory (EQ-i)*. Multi-Health Systems.
- EQ-i 2.0 Technical Manual

### TEIQue
- Petrides, K.V. (2009). *Technical Manual for the Trait Emotional Intelligence Questionnaires (TEIQue)*. London Psychometric Laboratory.
